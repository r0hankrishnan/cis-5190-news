# cis-5190-news
CIS 5190 Final Project: News Source Classification 

In this topic, you will work to scrape the News Headlines dataset and train on a Binary Text
Classification task using news headlines from two prominent news outlets: Fox News and
NBC News. The goal is to create some machine-learning models that classify news based
on their headlines. We will provide a baseline model with lower accuracies, 3815 URLs of
3815 news (so 2010 from FoxNews, 1805 from NBC) for you to start Headlines scraping, and a
screenshot of the first few lines of hidden test data that we will use to test your models. Your task will be to collect a dataset, process it, and experiment with various models to improve classification performance as best as you can. To show and summarize your improvement, in the final report, you will submit one or several line charts of your models’ metrics, including metrics of the baseline model.
